In the second year, I had already learned about MongoDB, PostgreSQL, Docker, and Node.js, but those were not part of the school's curriculum, which focused more on Laravel. Despite the challenges, I've completed quite a few projects and found the experience rewarding. My strong motivation serves as a foundation to keep pushing forward because learning has no limits.
